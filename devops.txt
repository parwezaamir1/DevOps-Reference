What is DevOps?
-- DevOps is a culture or practice which improves the ability of an organisation to deliver the applications
 - DevOps is a process of improving the applications delivery by insuring that there is a proper automation in 
   place, insuring that there is a quality maintained, continuous monitoring and finally there is a continuous
   testing in place
